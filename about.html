SKAPEGOHT is about an actual positive change for society.

SKAPEGOHT is an electronic dance music project created by Anthony Cofield. It aims to be controversial in practice in order to raise awareness to key issues around the United States and across the globe to create positive change and influence for all. Using the foundation of electronic dance music paired with lifelong influences from all genres such as Hip-Hop/Soul, Rock, Oldies, and Country to name some.

Although I have my own opinions and perspectives which guide the project forward, SKAPEGOHT is an ever evolving project and perspective that aims to find and seek truth by questioning and taking political action. It also aims to give voice and perspective to those who otherwise may not have a voice. The project is about freedom and equality as they go hand in hand.

What is meant by political activism?

All too long we have been lied to by our leaders and it is time for people to come together, uplift, and rise up, and it starts with our music & art.

EVERYONE is accepted in our community.

We are accepting of all races, genders, political ideologies, and religions.

As long as you are open minded to different perspectives and have love and respect in your heart, then this community is the one for you!
